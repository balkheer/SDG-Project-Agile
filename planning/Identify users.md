- Resident ( Primary User )

Residents are the people living in the building, representing the main users of the system. They will directly use the mobile application to meet their daily residential needs.


- Paying and viewing bills and invoices

- Receiving notifications and announcements

- Submitting maintenance requests

- Communicating with property management

- Posting updates within the community

- Using emergency features such as SOS alerts and fire alarm testing

The main purpose of this system is to enhance the safety, communication, and delivery of services to residents.


-  Property Management ( Secondary Users )


The Property Management team is responsible for managing the building operations, ensuring proper communication with residents and service providers.

- Sending announcements and notifications

- Managing bills and invoices

- Responding to residentsâ€™ inquiries and maintenance requests

- Reviewing community proposals or updates

- Monitoring emergency alerts such as SOS activations and fire alarms

The whole essence of this role lies in good building management and emergency response coordination.

- Maintenance Staff ( Secondary Users )

The Maintenance Staff members are the people who handle the maintenance issues within the building.

- Receiving assigned maintenance requests

- Updating task status (Pending, In Progress, Completed)

- Ensuring issues are resolved efficiently

- Reporting completed tasks to Property Management

This position helps in smooth functioning and prompt resolution of any issues.
